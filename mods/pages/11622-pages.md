**TL;DR (for those that can't make it to the bottom section):**

> This LUT_**IS**_compatible with other weather and lighting mods that use their own LUTs if you give Nova LUT load priority.

**Note:**

**This mod is not a reshade.**

With Nova 4, there is just one LUT (variants may be coming later).The achieve the look in the screenshots, it is recommended to use both the LUT and[Nova City 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/12490).

**Installation:**

Use Vortex (_recommended_) or manually install by just dragging and dropping the contents of the zip to the Cyberpunk 2077 root folder.

*   For GOG it should be something like "_C:\Games\Cyberpunk 2077\_[it goes here]
*   For Steam it should be something like "_C:\Games\Steam\steamapps\common\Cyberpunk 2077\_[it goes here]

**Compatibility:**

The LUT is **not**compatible with other LUTs. It is compatible with other mods.

Works with any version of the game (1.6, 2.0, 2.3+, etc).
