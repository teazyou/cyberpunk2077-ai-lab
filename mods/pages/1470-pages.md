WHAT?

A simple mod that cleans up Panam's face and removes her scar.

How to install:

CP 1.2+

Step 1: Download the mod .zip

Step 2: Unpack the .zip

Step 3: Go to "PATH TO CYBERPUNK\archive\pc\mod" (create mod folder if 

you have none), and copy paste the modded .archive file inside.

Step 4: Launch the game and enjoy

UP TO CP 1.1

Step 1: Download the mod .zip

Step 2: Unpack the .zip

Step 3: Go to "PATH TO CYBERPUNK\archive\pc\patch" (create patch folder if 

you have none), and copy paste the modded .archive file inside.

Step 4: Launch the game and enjoy

Alternative: Use Vortex 

How to uninstall:

Step 1: Delete the .archive from archive\pc\patch
