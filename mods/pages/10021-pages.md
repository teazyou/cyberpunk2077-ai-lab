Originally an addon for [Preem Optics](https://www.nexusmods.com/cyberpunk2077/mods/9278), Preem Scopes has been made its own mod simply due to the _**scope**(pun intended)_ of the project and how it differs from my vision for Preem Optics. This mod aims to remove most of the optical tint from scopes, remove scanlines and glitches, reduce the distortion and vignetting and remove the 3D HUD effect from some of the elements. This mod is a work in progress so, if you see a scope that has not yet been Preemified, please feel free to leave a comment or wait for another update.
