**In the name of immersion!**

What is this?

This mod replaces all female Tyger Claw heads with 9 custom heads made by me.

In total 26 NPCs get a new face, some Tyger Claw NPCs wear face covering helmets and so these NPCs, understandably, were not touched.

This gives the Tyger Claw female gang members a set of unique faces never seen anywhere else in the city, this is specifically done to increase immersion by reducing duplicate faces in the city, they are not meant to look better or more "attractive", that is not the goal here, variety is the goal.

Some appearances have also been edited to varying degrees.

**EXTRAS**

*   **Maiko Maeda** New look included in the mod (with alternative clothing choice)
*   **Roxanne Sumner** New look included in the mod
*   **Taki Kenmochi**New look included in the mod

**DISCLAIMER**This isn't a "make every NPC pretty" mod, this is for variation in faces, i don't care if you don't think they are good looking enough, that isn't the point here.
