# Fem V vs Masc V — what actually changes

**Main story: identical.** No gender-gated quests, no gender-gated endings, no branch in the Johnny/Relic arc. Lifepath (Nomad/Streetkid/Corpo) changes far more dialogue than gender does.

## The real difference: romance gates

Gate = **body type** (+ **voice** for the same-sex options). Genitals and lifepath are irrelevant.

| Partner | Requires |
|---|---|
| **Judy Álvarez** | feminine body + feminine voice |
| **River Ward** | feminine body |
| **Panam Palmer** | masculine body |
| **Kerry Eurodyne** | masculine body + masculine voice |

So fem V → Judy + River. Masc V → Panam + Kerry.
Gender-agnostic flings: Meredith Stout, Clouds joytoys (Skye / Angel).

Their **questlines are open to everyone** — only the romance branch inside them is gated. Pyramid Song, Following the River, Queen of the Highway, Off the Leash all play for any V; the kiss/night-together beat is what checks body+voice.

## Downstream

Romance partner colors the **epilogue**: post-ending calls, who shows up / leaves with V (e.g. Judy or Panam in the Star ending). No unique ending is gender-locked.

## Cosmetic-ish

- Different VA (Cherami Leigh / Gavin Drea) — perf differs, lines don't.
- Scattered NPC reaction lines, taunts, flirt options.
- Phantom Liberty: no romance at all, no gender branches.
