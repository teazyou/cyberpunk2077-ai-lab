# Run This Town — Dogtown Leader Choices (Phantom Liberty)

Side job, unlocks ONLY if Kurt Hansen dies in "Firestarter". Mr. Hands has V impersonate **Aguilar Nubiola** (legendary Cuban cartel assassin — behavioral imprint + suit + iconic revolver **Mancinella**, kept afterward) to pick Barghest's new boss.

## Candidates
- **Chester Bennett** — Hansen's chief aide, ex-military muscle. Hands' preferred pick. Secretly dealing with Arasaka (handler "Susan A." ordered him to grab Jago's contacts then kill Jago — Bennett = corpo puppet).
- **Jago Szabó** — Hansen's head accountant. Brains/money, no muscle. Hands calls him immature.

## Mid-quest branches
- Jago meet (construction site, Voodoo Boys ambush): MUST ask for proof of Bennett's Arasaka dealings, else quest ends early. Body check available to intimidate.
- Bennett car intercept: "forget Arasaka" -> boss fight; "Jago's not a threat" -> talk-down, no fight. Either way he submits, goes to wake.

## Final choice — Hansen's wake, Black Sapphire
| Choice | What happens | Hands | Reward |
|---|---|---|---|
| Back Bennett ("No. You do not get a new toy, Bennett") | Bennett executes Jago + his people, rules alone; all muscle, no brains -> Dogtown unstable | Displeased (wanted Jago alive) | ~8,000 |
| Back Jago ("A fair price, Jago. One you will pay") — needs high **Reflex check** (sources say 10–15) to shoot first | Bennett draws on Jago; V kills Bennett; Jago rules alone. Loot Bennett -> "Susan A." Arasaka shard | Very unhappy | ~6,000–8,000 |
| **Shared rule** ("Show your weapon" / "You would plunge Dogtown into chaos?") | Nobody dies; Bennett = figurehead muscle, Jago = advisor/brains; stable Dogtown | Happy | 10,000 (best) |

## Bottom line
- No effect on PL or base-game endings — flavor + eddies only.
- Shared rule = objectively best: max payout, Hands satisfied, both alive, stable Dogtown.
- Jago-solo = only path revealing Bennett's Arasaka-puppet shard on his corpse.
