# CrystalCoat — vehicle paint activation

Rayfield CrystalCoat = repaint owned vehicles in-game (added 2.11, expanded to most brands + TwinTone in 2.2).

## Activate (completes the "try CrystalCoat" message/quest after obtaining the car)
1. Enter (or sit on) the owned compatible vehicle.
2. **Hold Y** (PC keyboard default) / **hold d-pad LEFT** (controller) → CrystalCoat menu opens.
3. Pick Color 1, Color 2, headlight color (mode switch = hue/saturation), confirm → repaint animation plays, quest ticks off.

## Notes
- Owned vehicles only; won't trigger on stolen/NPC cars.
- TwinTone (2.2+): scan other vehicles (Kiroshi) to save their paint job, then apply to owned cars; some unique paints per model.
- Appearance refresh: re-summon car, toggle CrystalCoat, or refresh keybinding.
