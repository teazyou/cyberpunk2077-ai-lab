# Gigs (fixer contracts) & Side Jobs — counts, what's worth clearing

FR→EN: "Contrats" = Gigs (fixer missions) · "Missions annexes" = Side Jobs · "Crimes signalés NCPD" = NCPD Scanner Hustles (blue icons, separate from gigs).

## Gigs — totals

~86 base game + 9 new in Dogtown (PL) ≈ **95 total**. Mr. Hands' Dogtown counter shows 10 (includes recounted Pacifica gig "Two Wrongs Makes Us Right"). Exact per-fixer counts vary by source/patch (2.x reshuffled) — **in-game journal/map counter = authority**.

## Clear all gigs?

- Zero story impact — pure optional. Rewards: eddies, street cred, XP, per-fixer milestone cash + final iconic/vehicle above.
- 6 district achievements ("Little Tokyo", "The Jungle", "Mean Streets", "The Wasteland", "Greetings from Pacifica", "City Lights") need **all gigs + all NCPD hustles** in district. Failed-but-Completed journal entries still count.
- Verdict: completionist/trophy hunter → yes. Otherwise cherry-pick; Dogtown gigs highest reward density (iconic weapons), Regina's grind longest.

## Side jobs — totals & priorities

~80 base entries (many are 2-min micro-jobs / chain segments) + ~10 in PL. **Not all needed.** Priority tiers:

**Ending-gating (do before Nocturne Op55N1):**
- Panam chain: Riders on the Storm → With a Little Help from My Friends → Queen of the Highway → unlocks **The Star** + **Temperance** endings (+ Panam romance, male V).
- Johnny/Rogue chain: Chippin' In → Blistering Love → unlocks **The Sun** ending. Rewards: Malorian Arms 3516, Johnny's Porsche 911 (spare Grayson).
- **Secret ending (Don't Fear the Reaper)**: in Chippin' In at Johnny's grave pick: "The guy who saved my life" → "Nah, fucked that up too" → "What do you want from me?" → "OK. But as second chances go, this is your last." → "You were a real dick in the beginning" → "When you said you let down your friends…" → "Smasher biz really got to her." → "Yeah, I'll call Rogue." Then at ending balcony: wait ~5 min silent → "Let's hear this plan". Solo Arasaka raid, death = permanent.
- The Devil ending: not a side job — save Takemura during main job Search and Destroy (missable).

**Romances:** Judy chain (Ex-Factor → Talkin' 'Bout a Revolution → Pisces → Pyramid Song; fem V), River chain (I Fought the Law → The Hunt → Following the River; fem V), Kerry chain (via Second Conflict → Holdin' On → … ; male V).

**Story gems (no gates, just excellent):** Dream On (Peralez), Sinnerman, The Hunt, Space Oddity (→ eddies/painting).

**Reward chains:** Delamain (Epistrophy ×7 → Don't Lose Your Mind → free unique Delamain car), Beat on the Brat (fights), The Beast in Me (races → free car), Psycho Killer (17 cyberpsychos → Regina reward).

**Point of no return:** Nocturne Op55N1 (game warns explicitly). Post-credits you're dropped back to pre-PONR save → nothing permanently lost, but endings need chains done BEFORE.

## Level scaling (patch 2.0+)

Enemies + loot scale to V's level everywhere, main + side quests included, bounded min/max per zone → over-leveling from clearing side content does **not** trivialize/break story; bosses stay relevant. V still ends OP via iconics/cyberware/perks (scaling caps) → raise difficulty (Very Hard) if too easy. Level cap 50 base / 60 with PL.
