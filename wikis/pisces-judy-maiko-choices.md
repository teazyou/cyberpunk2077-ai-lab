# Pisces — Judy / Maiko choices & consequences

Side job **Pisces** (Judy chain: Automatic Love → Both Sides, Now → Ex-Factor → Talkin' 'Bout a Revolution → **Pisces** → Pyramid Song).
Setup: Maiko betrays Judy's plan — she staged the meet to burn Hiromi Sato in front of the other Tyger Claws bosses and take Clouds for herself, still under the Claws.

## The only hard lock: Maiko's money

| Choice | Result |
|---|---|
| **Accept Maiko's payment** (27,900 €$; Streetkid can haggle to 37,200) | Judy cuts ties, leaves Night City. **Pyramid Song never triggers. Judy romance locked.** No further Judy jobs. |
| **Everything else** | Pyramid Song fires ~24h later; romance available (fem body + fem voice V). |

Nothing in Pisces blocks main-story quests or any non-Judy questline.

## Branches

**A. Go along with Maiko, refuse the money** — no fight. Maiko replaces Hiromi, runs Clouds under Tyger Claws. Dolls' conditions no better than under Woodman; Tom & Roxanne alive but stuck. Judy disappointed, not hostile. Romance intact; she's visibly sad in Pyramid Song when she learns nothing changed.

**B. Declare independence → kill Hiromi + the 2 bosses, spare Maiko** — Judy thrilled in the moment (cheek kiss for fem V).

**C. Declare independence → kill the 3, then kill Maiko** — Judy shocked/sad, calls it unnecessary, but does **not** blame V. Romance intact. "Need time to digest" = scripted, not a fail state.

**Aftermath of B and C is the same**: Tyger Claws retaliate, **Tom is killed**, **Roxanne goes into hiding**, Clouds is reclaimed/shut. Killing Maiko changes tone, not outcome. (Some guides claim sparing Maiko leaves Clouds doll-run; the wiki/main sources say the Claws take it back either way.)

## Bottom line

No "save Clouds" ending exists — the dolls lose in every branch. The only durable variable is **Judy**: take the money → lose her; anything else → keep her (and Pyramid Song, the romance, her epilogue calls, and her role in the Star ending).
