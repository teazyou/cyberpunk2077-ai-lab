# Cellular Adapter (FR: Adaptateur cellulaire)

**Integumentary System cyberware**, perk-gated. FR perk name **DIFFÉRENT** = EN **Built Different**.

## Unlock chain

- Technical Ability tree, **Phenom tier (Tech 15)**, child of **License to Chrome** (which needs All Things Cyber).
- Perk "Built Different" unlocks purchase at any ripperdoc — and is **required for the implant to function** (installing without the perk = dead weight).

## What it does

No intrinsic ability — pure attribute scaler. Grants armor (~74–162 by tier/level) plus, **per Technical Ability point**:

| Bonus | Per Tech pt |
|---|---|
| Explosion resistance | +1.0% |
| Tech weapon damage | +0.5% |
| Health item recharge speed | +0.5% |
| Grenade recharge speed | +0.5% |

At Tech 20: +20% explosion resist, +10% tech dmg, +10% heal/grenade recharge → solid for tech-weapon/grenade builds, skippable otherwise.

Sold at ripperdocs; tier stock follows player level (see [[cyberware-item-tiers]]).
