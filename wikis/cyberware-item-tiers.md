# Cyberware & Item Tier Level Thresholds (Update 2.0+)

Since update 2.0, item tiers in loot and vendor/ripperdoc stock unlock at fixed **player level** thresholds (not Street Cred). Reaching a threshold makes that tier widely available; lower/higher tiers can still occasionally appear.

## Cyberware (ripperdocs)

| Tier | Appears from player level |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 1 |
| Tier 2 | 10 |
| Tier 3 | 20 |
| Tier 4 | 30 |
| Tier 5 | 40 |

## Weapons (vendors/loot)

| Tier | Appears from player level |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 1 |
| Tier 2 | 9 |
| Tier 3 | 17 |
| Tier 4 | 25 |
| Tier 5 | 33 |
| Tier 5++ | 51 (Phantom Liberty only) |

## Notes

- Installed cyberware can be upgraded to the next tier at any ripperdoc using item components of the matching tier (e.g. Tier 4 upgrade needs Tier 4 components).
- Tier 5+ / 5++ exist as upgrades above Tier 5; 5++ requires Phantom Liberty.
- Street Cred still gates some individual iconic items/vendors, but tier availability itself scales with character level.

Source: [CDPR support — Item Tiers in loot and vendors](https://support.cdprojektred.com/en/cyberpunk%20/pc/gameplay/issue/2593/item-tiers-in-loot-and-vendors)
